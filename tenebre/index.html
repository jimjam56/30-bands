Tenebre

A post/math rock from the US. Taking their cues from 33.3, Don Caballero and Owls, the instrumental trio Tenebre have established themselves in the movement of San Francisco post-punk spearheaded by bands such as Deerhoof and The Aislers Set. Anything but a typical indie post-rock act, Tenebre are more concerned with creating beautiful soundscapes than imitating their influences.
